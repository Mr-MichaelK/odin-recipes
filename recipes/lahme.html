Lahm bi Ajeen:

Description:

Lahm bi Ajeen is another form of Man'ooshe we serve. In arabic, it means "meat in dough".
What it really is though, is a flatbread with ground meat and onion on it.
The main ingredient is meat, in arabic lahm, hence why it gets the name.

An image of Lahm bi Ajeen manakeesh

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Ground meat
  • Finely chopped tomato
  • Finely chopped green pepper
  • Finely chopped red pepper
  • Finely chopped parsley
  • Pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Lemon

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Finely chop the green pepper, red pepper, onions, and parsley and place them in a bowl.
  3. Drain the bowl from excess water
  4. Mix the meat with the chopped vegetables, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and the juiced lemon.
  5. Spread the meat mixture onto the dough evenly
  6. Place the dough in a preheated oven for 10 minutes
  7. Serve with cold ayran

Remark: serving with ayran is recommended but optional.
