Zaatar:

Description:

Zaatar falls into the category of "Man'ooshe", which is a baked dough covered by a seasoning of your choice.

Zaatar, which is arabic for thyme, is the main ingredient of our people's favorite Man'ooshe, hence why it gets the name.

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Zaatar mix

Side note:

Do not confuse the Zaatar Man'ooshe with the Zaatar mix. The mix is used in the Man'ooshe!
