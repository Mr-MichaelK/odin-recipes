Zaatar:

Description:

Zaatar falls into the category of "Man'ooshe", which is a baked dough covered by a seasoning of your choice.
Zaatar, which is arabic for thyme, is the main ingredient of our people's favorite Man'ooshe, hence why it gets the name.

An image of four Zaatar manakeesh served with olives and cucumbers

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Zaatar spread

Side note:
Do not confuse the Zaatar Man'ooshe with the Zaatar spread. The spread is used in the Man'ooshe!

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Zaatar Spread
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Spread the Zaatar evenly on the dough
  4. Bake the dough in a preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes
  5. Serve with vegetables of choice

Note:

Typically I would recommend serving with olives, tomatoes, mint, and cucumbers.
